About Me

Hello, my name is Maira Padani. I am a student at Northeastern University studying Business Administration with a concentration in Marketing Analytics and a minor in Data Science. I am especially interested in the relationship between business, technology, and human behavior. What draws me to this field is the way data can help explain why people make certain choices and how companies can use that information to build better products, services, and customer experiences. I enjoy learning how organizations analyze patterns, test ideas, and make decisions based on evidence rather than assumptions. That combination of analytical thinking and real-world application is one of the main reasons I chose this area of study.

In my academic work, I have become more interested in consumer behavior, predictive analytics, artificial intelligence, and digital marketing. These topics show how businesses can better understand their audiences and respond in more relevant ways. I find it interesting that data can reveal patterns in customer preferences, engagement, and decision-making across websites, social media, apps, and streaming platforms. I also enjoy the problem-solving side of this work, especially when it involves connecting data insights to practical business strategy. Learning how data supports decision-making has helped me see marketing as both a creative and analytical field.

Outside of academics, I enjoy music, design, and exploring new technology. Music is one of my favorite interests because it combines creativity, emotion, and culture, but it also reflects how digital platforms use data to shape user experiences. Recommendation systems, personalization, and audience insights are all examples of how analytics influences everyday life. Through this website, I wanted to share more about who I am and the subjects that interest me most. I hope this site gives a clear introduction to my background and shows why I am excited about the intersection of marketing, analytics, technology, and consumer behavior.
